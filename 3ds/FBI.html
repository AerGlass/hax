CTRNAND Luma3DS


Power off your device
1.Press and hold (Start), and while holding (Start), power on your device. This will launch GodMode9

If you do not boot into GodMode9, ensure that GodMode9.firm is in /luma/payloads/ and that payloads is correctly spelled


If you are prompted to create an essential files backup, press (A) to do so, then press (A) to continue once it has completed


If you are prompted to fix the RTC date&time, press (A) to do so, then set the date and time, then press (A) to continue


2.Press (Home) to bring up the action menu
3.Select “Scripts…”
4.Select “GM9Megascript”
S.elect “Scripts from Plailect’s Guide”
6.Select “Setup Luma3DS to CTRNAND”
7.When prompted, press (A) to proceed
8.Press (A) to unlock SysNAND (lvl1) writing, then input the key combo given
9.Press (A) to continue

Backup essentials files


1.Select “Backup Options” 2.Select “SysNAND Backup” 3.Press (A) to confirm

If you get an error, make sure that you have at least 1.3GB of free space on your SD card


this will take some time

4.Press (A) to continue
5.Press (B) to return to the main menu
6.Select “Dump Options”
7.Select “Dump Boot9.bin & Boot11.bin”
8.When prompted, press (A) to proceed
9.Press (A) to continue
10.Press (B) to return to the main menu
11.Select “Exit”

12.Press (A) to relock write permissions if prompted
13.Navigate to [S:] SYSNAND VIRTUAL
14.Press (A) on essential.exefs to select it
15.Select “Copy to 0:/gm9/out”

If you see “Destination already exists”, press (A) on “Overwrite file(s)”


16.Press (A) to continue
17.Press (Home) to bring up the action menu
18.Select “Poweroff system” to power off your device
19.Insert your SD card into your computer
20.Copy __sysnand_###.bin, __sysnand_###.bin.sha, essential.exefs, _boot9_###.bin, and _boot11_###.bin from the /gm9/out/ folder on your SD card to a safe location on your computer
21.Make backups in multiple locations (such as online file storage)

These backups will save you from a brick and/or help you recover files from the NAND image if anything goes wrong in the future


23.Delete __sysnand_###.bin and __sysnand_###.bin.sha from the /gm9/out/ folder on your SD card after copying it
24.Reinsert your SD card into your device
25,Power on your device
